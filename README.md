# Graphics Test 5

> Raycasting engine test with OpenGL and GLFW.

## Controls

- `W` or `↑` to move forward.
- `S` or `↓` to move backward.
- `←` to turn left.
- `→` to turn right.
- `A` to strafe left.
- `D` to strafe right.
- `n` to toggle no-clip mode.
- `Tab` to toggle map.
- `Space` to interact.
- `Esc` to exit.
